Our Story

Our aim is to showcase Georgia's potential – not least to itself. To take pride in our history and our food, to tap into the bassline pulsing beneath Tbilisi, and the expertise of designers, artisans, chefs, and farmers that live and work throughout the country.

STORY

Rooms is a young and independent lifestyle hotel brand based in Georgia that has quickly emerged as the leader of distinctive hospitality experiences in the region. Since 2012, the Rooms has been shaping history and establishing Georgia as a global travel destination.

Rooms uniforms in Batumi.

All roads may end in Rome, but they go through Tbilisi first.

Take in the spacious rooms at Rooms Tbilisi, defined by dark wooden floorboards, elegant leather furnishings, and vintage light fittings.

Rooms Kazbegi is all about the view – and who you share it with, of course.

When Rooms Kazbegi opened in 2012, we wanted to step into the potential of this fantastic region by offering a hospitality experience to match the view.

It is no wonder that, in Soviet times, there was a sanatorium here, and it is from this modernist complex that Rooms has emerged.

Udabno farm: the largest regenerative agricultural venture in Europe comprising a staggering 20,000 hectares of land.

Georgian hospitality is at the core of the Rooms brand.

Our latest restaurant concept, Rubber Duck is everything you want from a classic diner – booth seating, pancakes, endless coffee – reinvented for Batumi's creative set.

UDABNO has become a key player in combating desertification, which nurtures biodiversity and promotes modern, sustainable agriculture in Georgia. The farm is also a steadfast supplier of almonds, berries, honey, and poultry within the group’s hotels and restaurants.

UDABNO almond farm

The lush rainforests surrounding our idyllic haven on the shores of the Black Sea, a boat ride from ROOMS Batumi.

Our famous mountain facing pool at Rooms Kazbegi.

Like all good things, the origins of the famous khinkali dumpling are contested. But some hold that their birthplace was right in Kazbegi.

Gergeti Trinity Church a wonderful monastery just a hike away from Rooms Kazbegi.

At our Tbilisi place, everyone comes through. It is a city within the city where local creatives meet, work and mingle.

A hike through Kazbegi mountains.
